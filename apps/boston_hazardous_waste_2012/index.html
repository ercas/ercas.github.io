partial list of hazardous waste generators in Boston

this map was generated from the 2017 version of the MassDEP hazardous waste generator list, which includes a list for all generators of hazardous waste in Massachusetts. the list was processed as follows:
  1. raw data (25641 records)
  2. subset to Town == Boston | Cambridge | Dorchester | Dorchester Center | Revere Beach | Roxbury | Somerville | South Boston (1867 records)
  3. run through U.S. Census geocoder and join on exact matches (1257 records)
click on a marker to get more information. information comes directly from the MassDEP file's column values.
